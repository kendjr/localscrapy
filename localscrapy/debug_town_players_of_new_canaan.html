Friday, May 2, 2025 – Town Players of New Canaan Reception and Play!
Connecticut Public is teaming up with the Town Players of New Canaan and the Carriage Barn Arts Center at Waveny Park for a delightful evening kicked off by an informative reception hosted by CT Public in the Carriage Barn Arts Center followed by the play, The Importance of Being Ernest.
The first 50 attendees to purchase tickets to the play will also enjoy the reception. Participants will also be able to enjoy an exceptional art exhibit on display, “Spectrum 2025: Juried Contemporary Art Exhibition,” at the Carriage Barn Arts Center.
Check out other plays offered by the Town Players of New Canaan – The Best Christmas Pageant Ever, Terra Nova, and the Shawshank Redemption
